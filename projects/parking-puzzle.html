Parking Puzzle is inspired by the many times I've had to rewind and retry when parallell parking. I made it in one week for Brackeys Game Jam 2020.2. The theme was "Rewind" and Parking Puzzle ranked #80 out of 1847 entries.

The game is about cleverly arranging various blocks to guide the car into the parking spot.
