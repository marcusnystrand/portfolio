Livia Funeral Agency

Concept & visual identity for a fictional funeral agency. With Livia I wanted to explore what rationality in this very non-rational business would look like. The idea is simple; you apply for the service, get the body cremated and finally get the ashes deliviered to your door.

Tone & style in advertising.


Website, where you buy the service.


Cremation invitation.


Ashes, folder & business cards, delivered to your door.


The ashes are vacuum packed.


The folder talks about dealing with death in practical and emotional ways.
