SAVE THE DINOS!
Fierce meteorites hail down on Dino Land and the Dinos need to repopulate quickly before they all go down in history. Click meteorites to shoot them with your laser and click Dinos to make them dash in their opposite direction. Made by me in 72 hours for the game jam Ludum Dare 46.

Play the game here!
Made in Unity game engine.
